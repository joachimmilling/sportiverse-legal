Why We Exist

The platform where your sport lives

Sportiverse is the go-to platform for organizing your sport. Self-serve, mobile-first, and zero-friction. Norwegian grassroots football is the starting point. The ambition is every sport, everywhere.

Equal parts coaches, players, and fans — three audiences, equally served.

Data First

Every feature produces or consumes data. Ratings come from match events, not opinion. Standings come from results, not manual entry.

Always Fun

A sports app, not a spreadsheet. Live scoring that feels like watching a match. Celebrations on goals, wins, and milestones.

Frontier Technology

Best available tools, not most familiar. The team is small — leverage matters more than convention.
